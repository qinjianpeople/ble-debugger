隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025
感谢您使用本应用。本隐私政策用于说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。
Thank you for using this App. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.
1. 收集的信息
1. Information Collected
本应用仅请求以下权限：
The App only requests the following permissions:
  • 蓝牙权限：用于扫描和连接蓝牙设备，不会存储或上传任何蓝牙设备信息。
  • Bluetooth Access: To scan and connect to Bluetooth devices. No device information is stored or uploaded.
  • 联网权限：仅用于从 App Store 获取内购信息。
  • Internet Access: To retrieve in-app purchase information from the App Store.
2. 使用信息
2. Use of Information
我们不会收集、存储或上传任何用户数据，所有操作均在设备本地完成。
We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All processing happens locally on your device.
3. 数据安全
3. Data Security
应用未集成第三方 SDK，不传输用户数据，不保存任何用户信息。
No third-party SDKs are integrated. No data is transmitted or retained.
4. 第三方服务
4. Third-party Services
本应用可能使用 Apple 内购服务，详情请参阅 Apple 隐私政策。
This App may use Apple's in-app purchase services. Please refer to the Apple Privacy Policy.
5. 用户权利
5. User Rights
由于我们不收集任何信息，您无需担心数据访问或删除问题。
Since no data is collected, there is no need for data access or deletion requests.
6. 联系方式
6. Contact

If you have any questions, please submit an issue on our GitHub repository.
